The basics

My gender is male (perhaps the facial hair and name tipped you off?), and I was born on 3 July 1970. I am very happily divorced, and am in a serious relationship with a lovely nurse whom I have known for years. I have three daughters and five granddaughters. I've given up on the idea of having a son or grandson, and now claim my nephew as my one and only, although he is now grown, and an Embassy Guard in the US Marine Corps in Austria.

I have been recently diagnosed with MS (multiple sclerosis). Although it took me by surprise and has brought many changes to my lifestyle, it is also a source of inspiration for me to stay active and take better care of myself. Sometimes I have to walk with a cane, sometimes I have to use a wheelchair, but always I am grateful for my health, family, and friends.

I am a software developer, and have been for over 30 years. My parents bought me a TRS-80 Color Computer 2, by Tandy / Radio Shack, when I was 14 years old, and I've been hooked on coding ever since. That "trash 80" or "coco" as it is affectionately known, was a turning point in my life. I started coding from the manual that came with it, and I have been writing code ever since. Currently, I am working on a destkop application package for the automotive industry, and the software is now in beta testing. I hope to have it in production use within the next six months.

Some people go through life without working in the field of their passion. I'm blessed to have found mine and worked in it for almost all my life. I cannot imagine enjoying a caereer more than software development.
