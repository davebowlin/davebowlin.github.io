More random things about me

I am a huge fan of the Robert E. Howard Conan the Barbarian novels, stories, and comics. The movies? They're okay, at best. Queue the robotic Arnold voice: "I'll be back!" Douglas Adams is my favorite novelist, although he hitched his way to Heaven years ago. Ford Prefect, Arthur Dent, and Marvin have been part of my life for years, and I even have a tattoo of "Mostly Harmless" on my shoulder. Do you know where your towel is? If so, you're a hoopy frood, and the Answer is 42.

I have written over 100 short stories and novellas, although the "great American novel" has eluded me thus far. Writing short stories to amuse myself is a wonderful way to stay young, creative, and imaginative. Most of them have been published at one time or another, mostly online.

I have a book of short stories available on Amazon, and I was the editor of a collection, available at Barnes and Nobles online. I have also published several nonfiction pieces as well, online and print, in such magazines as Writer's Digest, Would That It Were, Foxfire Magazine, and several newspaper and technical editorials.

I've been playing Dungeons & Dragons since 1993 with family and friends, and I build almost all of the props for our sessions myself, using a variety of tools, materials, and techniques I've learned over the years.

Although I have tried for a few decades now, I still suck at playing the harmonica.
